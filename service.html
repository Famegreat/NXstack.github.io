Services

  • Video Conferencing: XStack can offer high-quality video conferencing with features such as screen sharing, multiple video feeds, and customizable layouts. It should support both one-on-one meetings and larger group meetings.

  • Audio Conferencing: Ensure reliable and clear audio quality for participants. Features like noise cancellation and the ability to dial in via phone should be included.

  • Screen Sharing: Allow participants to share their screens, making it easy to collaborate on documents, presentations, or demonstrate software. Virtual Whiteboard: Provide a virtual whiteboard where participants can draw, write, and brainstorm together in real-time. This is especially useful for creative discussions and educational purposes.
